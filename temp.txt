Lapikud VIM Workshop takes place this Wednesday, 1. April at 18:00-19:30 at Lapikute kontor.

Wizard Joosep wants to know if you're interested in learning the best thing since buttered popcorn. We'll be looking at what actually is VIM seeing what it can do and learning the basic of using it, while also being mesmerized by seeing the pinicle of it's usage. Topics covered: Basic movement, editing and searching through text, how to exit vim, configuring vim  and a bonus topic if students want to know how to do something specific in vim.

Participating will get the best student a prize. 

The workshop is free. Please register to let The head Wizard know how many people to expect in person.
